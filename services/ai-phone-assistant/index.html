A 24/7 Voice-Trained Assistant That Never Sleeps.

Capture every call. Our custom voice-trained AI Phone Assistants answer inbound calls, follow up with outbound leads in under 30 seconds, qualify queries, and schedule appointments on autopilot.

24/7 Inbound/Outbound Human-Like Voices Speed-to-Lead CRM Sync

Core Capabilities & Deliverables

✓
Inbound Customer Support: An outbound-ready bot that answers property details, office hours, and client queries instantly.
✓
Instant Outbound Follow-Up: Call new website signups in under 30 seconds, qualifying interest and scheduling calls.
✓
Database reactivation bots: Automatically call thousands of older leads to reactivate interest and generate warm bookings.
✓
Live Agent Call Transfers: Smart routing that transfers qualified callers directly to your cell phone when a hot opportunity is detected.

What Every Project Includes

  • Custom conversation script mapping custom triggers and FAQs.
  • Premium ultra-realistic AI voice selection (natural tone and pauses).
  • Dedicated phone number setup with custom caller ID.
  • Integration with Google Calendar and your CRM.
  • SMS text forwarding for booked appointments and user summaries.
  • Call recording log dashboard with sentiment analysis tags.
  • Regular voice optimization revisions post-launch.

Detailed Integration Process

  1. Script Blueprint & Tone Setup: We map out the voice assistant conversation path, FAQs, and tone guidelines.
  2. Voice Training & Testing: We program the voice variables, test pauses, verify caller identity, and check pronunciation.
  3. API & Pipeline Sync: We hook up CRM tags, SMS forwarders, calendars, and live transfer lines.
  4. Launch & System Audit: We activate the system, run test calls, verify data collection, and go live.
  5. Performance Optimization: We analyze call recordings, tweak prompts, and train the model for higher accuracy.
