Detailed Integration Process

  1. Lead Sync & Script Design: We hook up CRM tags, compile scripts, and establish target criteria.
  2. Database Warm-Up Campaign: We deploy sms/email sequences to filter out invalid numbers and gauge intent.
  3. Outbound Calling & Qualify: Outbound outreach agents or voice bots reach out to schedule appointments.
  4. Calendar Booking Alert: The lead selects an open time slot, sending an invitation straight to your inbox.
  5. Call Handoff & Prep: We send you qualification notes (timeline, budget) so you know exactly who you are calling.
