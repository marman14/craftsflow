Home / Services / Lead Follow-Up

Never Let a Lead Go Cold. Automatically.

It takes an average of 8 touchpoints to convert a raw lead. We build automated multi-day follow-up sequences across SMS, email, and calling tags to nurture leads until they book a call.

Multi-Channel Drips High Response Copy Instant Triggers A/B Tested Hooks

Core Capabilities & Deliverables

✓
SMS Conversational Campaigns: Two-way SMS scripts that ask qualifying questions and guide leads to booking calls.
✓
Drip Email Sequences: Educate prospects with automated emails detailing client case studies, testimonials, and authority reports.
✓
Multi-Channel Workflows: Sync SMS, email, and tasks so they trigger in a structured order (e.g., SMS at min 5, Email at hour 2).
✓
Long-Term Nurture Flows: Stay top-of-mind with monthly check-ins for leads who are not ready to purchase immediately.

What Every Project Includes

  • Custom drip sequence structure mapping 14-30 days of touchpoints.
  • Copywriting for up to 10 email steps and 6 SMS scripts.
  • Dynamic tags injection (inserting client name, listing location automatically).
  • CRM trigger rules setup (triggers when tag is added or form is submitted).
  • Deduplication filters ensuring leads do not receive duplicate messages.
  • Opt-out compliance configuration (unsubscribes, STOP tags).
  • Conversion rate tracking setup.

Detailed Integration Process

  1. Funnel & Audit Discovery: We analyze your current response times and map out ideal customer paths.
  2. Copywriting & Script Sets: Our team drafts engaging emails and short, conversational SMS scripts.
  3. Pipeline Implementation: We build the campaigns, configure delays, and organize tags inside your CRM.
  4. Automation Testing: We trigger test leads, verifying SMS deliveries, email layouts, and stops.
  5. Launch & Tracking Sync: We go live, tracking reply rates and optimizing copy elements to improve results.
