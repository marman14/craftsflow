Builder / Developer Case Study

$3.2M in Residential Presales in 60 Days

How we built a high-converting project microsite, launched Facebook buyer campaigns, and sold out 80% of a development before groundbreaking.

Client Dallas Modern Homes LLC
Location Dallas, TX Metro
Services Used Project Website, Facebook Ads, 3D Virtual Media
Investment Return 15x ROI
$3,200,000 in early commitments

By capturing 180+ qualified investor and residential buyer leads, the development company sold 8 out of 14 units before pouring the building foundations.

The Challenge

Developers face massive carrying costs waiting for units to sell post-construction. Dallas Modern Homes needed to secure early reservations to satisfy bank financing requirements and lower risk, but had no digital footprint for the new project.

Our Custom Strategy

We built a project launch funnel in under 10 days:

  • Project Landing Page: Created a custom microsite displaying floorplans, high-quality 3D unit renders, and booking buttons.
  • Targeted Lead Campaigns: Ran Meta ads targeting local investors and buyers looking for high-end modern residential homes.
  • Instant Pipeline Alerts: Connected the form submissions to automated CRM flows that immediately texted brochure details to prospects.

The Results & Verification

Verification metrics:

  • 182 inbound buyer registrations.
  • 8 early unit sales contracts signed.
  • Fully satisfied lender presales requirement in 60 days.
