100% Sold Out in 5 Months

How immersive Matterport 3D tours and targeted real estate buyer campaigns sold out a multi-family development ahead of schedule.

Client Vanguard Development Group
Location Dallas, TX
Services Used 360° Virtual Tours, Email Marketing, Paid Social
Investment Return 14x ROI
All 24 Units Under Contract

By capturing over 320 high-intent developer leads and routing them through a personalized booking system, Vanguard sold all units in 150 days.

The Challenge

Vanguard had a 24-unit luxury condo project under construction but faced heavy competition from larger developers. Traditional brochure site pages and listings were not driving early contract commitments.

Our Custom Strategy

We created a fully digital sales experience:

  • 3D Digital Twins: Completed high-end scans of the model unit, creating interactive virtual walkthroughs.
  • Investor Campaigns: Target-marketed out-of-state buyers looking for Dallas investment assets.
  • Nurture Sequences: Ran weekly email sequences highlighting construction milestones and remaining unit listings.
