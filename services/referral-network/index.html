Home / Services / Referral Network

Unlocking Out-of-Market Client Referrals.

Up to 30% of business for top agents comes from referrals. We build and automate campaigns targeting out-of-market agents in feeder cities to capture client referrals.

Feeder City Targeting Agent-to-Agent Outreach Calendar Bookings CRM Database Sync

Core Capabilities & Deliverables

✓
Feeder City Campaigns: Identify cities where residents are moving from, and target top-producing agents in those markets.
✓
Bespoke Outbound outreach: Polished SMS, email, and social sequences introducing your market statistics and referral fee structures.
✓
Referral Directory Setup: Build private networks and directories to share listing info and client needs with out-of-market partners.
✓
Referral Pipeline Analytics: Track incoming queries, booked coordination calls, and closed transactions to measure campaign success.

What Every Project Includes

  • Feeder city migration data analysis and target agent lists.
  • Copywriting for outbound referral introduction sequences.
  • Automated email and LinkedIn outreach workflow setup.
  • Direct scheduling link setup (booking coordination calls).
  • Follow-up sequence design (updates on local market trends).
  • Opt-out list management to protect email sender reputation.
  • Monthly campaign performance dashboard reviews.

Detailed Integration Process

  1. Feeder Market Analysis: We research migration patterns to target the exact cities sending the most relocations to your area.
  2. Agent List Curation: We compile contact details for top-producing agents in those target feeder cities.
  3. Campaign Copy Design: We write outbound sequences outlining your local credentials and referral terms.
  4. Workflow Automation Setup: We configure outreach workflows and trigger email steps inside your CRM.
  5. Activation & Meeting Bookings: We launch campaigns. Agents click your link to book introductory coordination calls.
